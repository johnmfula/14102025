About Us

Learn more about the UCZ Bemba Hymn Book digital platform

Our Mission

To make Bemba hymns accessible to everyone, anywhere, at any time.

This digital platform is designed to preserve and share the rich heritage of Bemba hymns from the United Church of Zambia (UCZ) hymn book. We believe that worship through music should be accessible to all members of the Bemba-speaking community, whether at home, in church, or on the go.

What We Offer

  • 234 Complete Hymns - Full collection of Bemba hymns with all verses
  • Easy Search - Find hymns quickly by number or title
  • Mobile Friendly - Access hymns on any device, anywhere
  • Favorites System - Save and organize your favorite hymns
  • Share Features - Share hymns with friends and family
  • Free Access - No registration or payment required

Our Vision

We envision a future where:

  • Every Bemba-speaking Christian has easy access to hymns for worship
  • Traditional hymns are preserved for future generations
  • Technology serves to strengthen faith and community
  • Worship resources are freely available to all, regardless of location or economic status
